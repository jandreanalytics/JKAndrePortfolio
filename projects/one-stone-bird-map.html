Project Overview

The ONE STONE BIRD MAP was developed to shed light on the underreported issue of avian mortality in urban areas, particularly from window collisions and outdoor cats. By geographically visualizing these incidents, the project transforms abstract statistics into a tangible representation of their impact, making the issue more accessible and compelling to the general public. This approach fosters awareness, informs conservation efforts, and highlights the need for action to mitigate human-driven threats to bird populations in the DMV region.
