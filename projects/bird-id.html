Project Overview

AVIAN VISION ML represents a significant advancement in automated bird species identification, achieving 91.23% accuracy across 117 North American bird species. Built on a modified EfficientNet-B0 architecture with custom attention mechanisms, this model was trained on 292,500 images (2,500 per species) and transforms the challenging task of fine-grained bird classification into a reliable, accessible tool for both research and conservation applications.
